Maintain the index

  • Source: visible launchpad rows come from data/navigation.json.

  • Update: promote only clean, user-facing rows into the curated navigation file.
  • Placeholder: use needsUrl: true when the product is known but the embedded launch URL still needs review.

Session prep

Sign in once to the systems that own protected products before opening launchpad links. This does not bypass permissions; it keeps normal ArcGIS and Power BI sessions warm so users are not challenged on every click.
