What this is

One launchpad for American Red Cross Florida regional operating products — maps, dashboards, situational awareness, weather, and reference tools. Every tile opens its live product.

How to use it

  • Browse: click any tile to open its product.
  • Search: press / or click the search box, type, then press Enter for the best match.
  • Filter: narrow tiles by destination — Experience, ArcGIS, Power BI, or External.

Maintain the index

  • Source: visible launchpad rows come from data/navigation.json.
  • Audit: authenticated Assistant extracts go to data/extracted-links.json.
  • Update: promote only clean, user-facing rows into the curated navigation file.
  • Placeholder: use needsUrl: true when the product is known but the embedded launch URL still needs review.
  • Review: open the .
  • Edit: open the .
  • Check: search the changed item and confirm the link opens correctly.
